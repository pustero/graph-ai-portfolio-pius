Professional Profile

I am a Physician with over 20 years of clinical and research experience in infectious disease programs globally. My work has spanned Europe, Latin America, Africa, and Asia, focusing on high-impact areas like HIV and Tuberculosis.

Driven by the potential for technology to transform healthcare, I am currently investigating how Generative AI can automate research synthesis, enhance grant writing, and streamline clinical documentation at HUG (Geneva University Hospitals).

My approach combines the scientific rigor of a PhD with the agility of modern AI tools to solve complex challenges in public health.
